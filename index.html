DABAS SECURITY & ALLIED SERVICES PVT. LTD.

DABAS started its operations in 2011 under the guidance of Mr. Radhey Shyam Solanki. The burgeoning entity of 2011 has blossomed into a fine tuned, technologically advanced and timely delivered capable organization that is DABAS SECURITY & ALLIED SERVICES PVT.LTD. We are amongst the leading provider of the finest investigation services along with security, LOADING-UNLOADING and housekeeping services to legal, Business and Financial Organization in the Government, Public Sector, Private Companies and Individuals. DABAS Systems is proud to have earned a reputation for excellence by consistently delivering resources and results that exceed our partner's expectations. We believe it is because of this commitment to quality, that most of our business continues to come from referrals, the heart of any good business.

We believe that trust must be earned by giving 100%. Our commitment is your assurance.
